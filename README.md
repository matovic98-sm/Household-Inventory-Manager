# Household-Inventory-Manager
A chatbot that allows users to manage an inventory and a grocery list.
Users can manually enter information about items in their household inventory, such as the item’s name, the quantity, and the expiration date. They can also view, add, or delete items from both their inventory and their grocery list, and when items in their inventory expire or run out, they are automatically entered into their grocery list.
